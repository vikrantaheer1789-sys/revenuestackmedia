Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RevenueStack Media (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses and protects information when you visit this website or contact us.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, business email, company, phone number and details submitted through a contact form.

We may also collect basic technical and usage information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages and website interactions through analytics or security tools.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and evaluate partnership opportunities.
  • To operate, maintain and improve the website.
  • To protect the website against fraud, abuse and security threats.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality, analytics and marketing measurement. Where legally required, we will request consent before enabling non-essential cookies.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, process inquiries, host data, perform analytics or maintain security, subject to appropriate contractual safeguards.

5. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including legal, accounting or dispute-resolution requirements.

6. International processing

Because we may work with service providers and partners in different countries, information may be processed outside your country of residence. We take reasonable steps to protect information during such processing.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection concerning your personal information. Contact us to exercise an applicable right.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no internet transmission or storage method is completely secure.

9. Third-party links

This website may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@revenuestackmedia.com.
